Batad Rice Terraces

Description

Batad Rice Terraces also known as Banaue Batad Rice Terraces is a green rice terraces set in a village with a population that only manages to reach about 1,500 people.

While you’re here, you can stay with a family for a homestay opportunity and hike to a nearby waterfall with a friendly guide. See a side of the Philippines through the eyes of the people who life and thrive here.

Restaurants nearby

7th Heaven’s Cafe

Transportation Tips

You can ride a bus that is direct super deluxe service from Manila to Banaue. The bus departs from Quezon (greater Manila area) to the village of Banaue in the Ifugao province. This is the Super Deluxe bus service, seating 35 passengers, which means the bus is modern and very comfortable.
